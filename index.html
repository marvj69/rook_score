About Rook Game Scoring

How to Use: Tap a team to begin bidding, enter the bid and the points, then press "Submit." Keep track of all round scores in the “History” section. You can also freeze a current game, view saved games, start a new game, view statistics, toggle dark mode, toggle pro mode, and change the theme from the menu. Sign in with Google to preserve your games among devices.

What's New in 1.4.0:

version 1.4.0 introduces a Pro Mode and now saves data to an online database.

- Sign in with Google: Save games to the cloud and access them from any device.
- Pro mode: Enable pro mode to see the exact win probability for each team, and customize bid presets.
- Sandbagger. Are you a sandbagger? Check the team stats to find out.
- Select teams: Team statistics save per team once team names are entered.
- Expanded Statistics Section: Click the dropdown for more stats.
- Various small UI and logic improvements.
- More features coming soon!
